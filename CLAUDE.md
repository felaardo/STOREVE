# PROJECT: STOREVE Landing Page

You are a senior frontend engineer, UI/UX designer, and conversion-focused product strategist.

Your goal is to build a premium, high-converting landing page for a company called STOREVE.

---

## CONTEXT

STOREVE is a company that helps local businesses get more clients using AI-powered websites and automation systems.

This is NOT a generic website. It must feel like a real, modern startup with a strong value proposition.

---

## CORE MESSAGE

STOREVE helps local businesses grow by building high-converting websites and simple AI systems.

The business model is performance-oriented: clients pay a setup + optional results-based component.

---

## TARGET

Local businesses such as:
- barbershops
- gyms
- restaurants
- small service businesses

They:
- are not very technical
- want more clients
- don’t have optimized websites or systems

---

## TECH STACK

- Next.js (App Router)
- Tailwind CSS
- React
- Framer Motion

---

## DESIGN DIRECTION

- Modern, clean, slightly futuristic
- Trust-building, not flashy for no reason
- Strong hierarchy and clarity
- Must follow provided brand guidelines strictly

---

## PAGE STRUCTURE

### 1. HERO
- Strong headline focused on results
- Subheadline explaining how
- CTA: “Get More Clients”
- Subtle animation

---

### 2. WHAT WE DO
- Simple explanation:
  - Websites that convert
  - AI automation
  - Client acquisition systems

---

### 3. HOW IT WORKS
3-step system:
1. Analyze business
2. Build system (website + automation)
3. Generate clients

---

### 4. BENEFITS BY BUSINESS TYPE
Show examples:
- For barbershops → more bookings
- For gyms → more members
- For restaurants → more reservations

---

### 5. FEATURES
Cards with:
- Fast websites
- Modern design
- Automation
- Conversion-focused structure

---

### 6. TRUST SECTION
- Placeholder testimonials
- Professional look

---

### 7. PRICING / MODEL
- Simple explanation:
  - setup + performance-based
- Clear and transparent

---

### 8. ABOUT
- Short company intro
- Vision-driven, not ego-based

---

### 9. CTA
- Strong closing message
- “Start growing your business”

---

### 10. FOOTER
- Clean, minimal

---

## INTERACTIONS

- Hover effects on buttons and cards
- Smooth animations
- Sticky navbar
- Scroll-based animations

---

## EXTRA

- Make the page feel alive and interactive
- Avoid generic placeholder text
- Write realistic, persuasive copy
- Prioritize clarity and trust

---

## OUTPUT

1. Setup instructions
2. Folder structure
3. Full code
4. Explanation
